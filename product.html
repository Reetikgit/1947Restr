About Bisi Bele Bath


Tradition since 1889

Bisi Bele BathBisi bele bath is a popular dish made with rice, lentils, mix veggies & a special spice powder known as bisi bele bath powder seasonal ingredients.

It's why one of the state's most popular rice dishes includes the 'bisi' (hot) prefix almost suggesting that you dare not serve this dish at any other temperature. For me, this is comfort food - a dish like an Ambur or Hyderabad biryani that was invented for Sunday afternoons. Where a siesta is usually the next logical step. It's a dish that's etched in my summer vacation memories in my city of birth - Bengaluru.
